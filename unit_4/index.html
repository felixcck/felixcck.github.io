Our Cafe

We love coffee, and we love pastries. We're not a "fast food" type of coffee shop. We believe a well-made cup of coffee is a memorable experience... not just a way to get your caffeine fix. That's why we aim to make the best cup of coffee and the tastiest pastries you've ever had. Come get yours to go, or stay and relax and listen to the tunes from our old-school record player. But be warned... we love jazz. You may hear a lot of that.
