Our Pastries


As much as we love coffee, we love pastries equally. Not only are they delicious but the challenge of making them perfect makes them even more special. It was only natural then to have our friend, Mandy, provide all of the mouth-watering pastries that fill the coffee shop. Both savoury and sweet, our pastries are baked in our own kitchen, fresh every day.


Mandy studied baking in Paris at the prestigious Le Cordon Bleu, and apprenticed at several fine patisseries in the city thereafter. When she returned home, we knew we had to grab her and her talents.


Each delectable treat is a classic, or something new stirred up by Mandy in the kitchen. We are always excited to see what she comes up with, and we will be sure to update you when she's ready to share it. Follow us on Twitter and Instagram to stay posted for Mandy's pastry surprises.
