Our Cafe


On the corner of Lawton Street and 43rd Avenue in the Sunset district of San Francisco - steps away from the edge of the continent - is our little cafe. It doesn't look like anything special from the outside, but inside you'll feel like you've just come home. We designed our cafe to reflect the style that we love and enjoy spending time in - not the rustic and nostalgic vibe that we've often seen - but a more modern design like atmosphere, infused with art, plants, and jazz.


We roast our beans in house to add to the aroma filling the cafe. We're not a "fast food" type of coffee shop. We believe a well-made cup of coffee is a memorable experience... not just a way to get your caffeine fix. That's why we aim to make the best cup of coffee you've ever had.
